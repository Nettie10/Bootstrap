Danny Phantom

Actors: Danny Phantom played by David Kaufman Vald played by Martin Mull Sam played by Grey DeLisie Dark Danny played by Eric Roberts Tucker Foley played by Rickey Cree Summer played by Valerie Gray

Danny Fenton was once your typical kid until he accidentally blew up his parents laboratory and became a ghost-hunting superhero. He straddles with living two different life. He frequently saves his town and the world from ghost attacks while being a shy freshmen at Casper Hight. The first episode was dropped 2004 April 3. It was originally supposed to be called Danny Phantom and the Specter Detectors. Throughout his adventures, Danny made a number of allies, including his half-ghost female clone and cousin, Dani Phantom, a Yeti-like ghost named Frostbite, the Master of Time by the name of Clockwork, a werewolf ghost named Wulf and Pandora, the owner of Pandora's Box. Fun fact Danny Phantom was created by Butch Hartman, who is the creator of The Fairly OddParents.
